ABOUT US

Two friends. One kitchen. A lot of questions.

We're Samy and Renske. A few years ago we were both stuck in the same loop: bloated after lunch, exhausted by 3 p.m., and confused by food advice that contradicted itself everywhere we looked. Gimme is what we built to get out of it.

How it started

It began with a phone camera propped against a stack of cookbooks, filming our first kimchi fried rice in Samy's tiny kitchen. We weren't trying to build a brand, we were just two friends swapping recipes that finally made us feel good.

The more we cooked, the more patterns we saw: fermented foods calmed our stomachs, warm iron-rich meals made period week bearable, and enough protein at breakfast killed the 3 p.m. crash. Nothing magic, just consistent, boring, effective basics.

Gimme is those basics, organized: recipes that work, education without the hype, and tools that help you notice how food actually makes you feel.

What we believe

🥬

Food first

No powders, no detox teas, no miracle claims. Real meals beat supplements almost every time.

🔍

Honest over hype

We say "we don't know" when we don't know, and we'll never sell you certainty that doesn't exist.

📅

Consistency wins

One perfect week changes nothing. Small habits you can keep; that's what actually works.
